Privacy statement

This document describes how information is handled in connection with the mobile game distributed on the Apple App Store under that title. It is written for players and families in ordinary language.

Summary

The product is a self-contained puzzle experience on your device. You are not asked to register a separate in-game identity to play. The distributor’s storefront may show an “App Privacy” nutrition label; if that label lists no data types collected by the app, that summary is the high-level reference alongside this page.

Data on your device

Like many casual titles, the software may keep gameplay-related details on the handset itself—for example progress within levels, preferences you set in the app, or similar state needed to resume play. That information lives in the app’s sandbox unless you use a system feature that explicitly syncs or backs it up according to your device settings.

Removing the application or erasing its data through iOS settings clears that local content from the device in the usual way.

Advertising

The age and content disclosures on the store listing indicate that advertising may appear. Ad networks, if present, operate under their own technical rules; you can limit ad tracking at the system level using Apple’s privacy controls for your device.

Apple and Game Center

Installing, updating, or restoring purchases happens through Apple’s infrastructure, which has its own agreements and privacy notices. Optional social features such as leaderboards, when enabled, rely on Apple’s Game Center services and the Apple ID you already use on the device.

Younger players

The title is intended for a general audience. Guardians can use Screen Time, Ask to Buy, and other parental tools built into iOS and the App Store to supervise installs, in-app activity, and purchases on shared hardware.

Changes

Gameplay, platform requirements, or regulatory guidance can evolve. When a material change occurs, this text may be revised so it continues to reflect how the product is meant to behave.

✦

Nothing in this overview replaces mandatory notices from Apple or rights that apply where you live. For account-level requests that involve your Apple ID, use Apple’s privacy and data tools in addition to managing the app on your devices.
